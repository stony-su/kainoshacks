Intro to Shanghai

Shanghai is a global financial hub and one of the most populous cities in the world, known for its modern skyline and deep cultural heritage. The city blends cutting-edge architecture with traditional Chinese elements. The Bund, with its historic colonial buildings, contrasts with the futuristic skyscrapers of Pudong.
